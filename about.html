About us

Who we are

In 2019, Student Assist is an online learning platform that allows students to learn at their own pace, test themselves and get ahead with our expert
tutors anytime and anywhere.By offering short, engaging video lessons, visualized learning journeys, continuous assessment and performance
analyt ics, we are changing the way students learn outside the classroom.


Our Content

From creative video animation to solid question banks, we invest in producing the highest-quality content for our students. Each piece of cont-
ent we create goes through a rigorous process of quality evaluation and audit by subject matter experts to ensure that only the best is delivered.
We currently offer the Pakistan National curriculum for grades 7-12. Subjects include: Math, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Arabic and English.


Our Mission

We are striving to make high-quality academic education more accessible for students across the World.


Our Future Vision
